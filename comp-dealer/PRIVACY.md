# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 13, 2026**

## Overview

Comp Dealer ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

- ❌ No user tracking
- ❌ No analytics
- ❌ No personal information storage
- ❌ No device identifiers
- ❌ No third-party services

## Local Storage

All data created in Comp Dealer is stored **locally on your device only**:
- Compliments you write
- Requests you make
- Any user-generated content

This data is:
- ✅ Never sent to our servers
- ✅ Never shared with third parties
- ✅ Deleted when you uninstall the app
- ✅ Under your complete control

## No External Communication

Comp Dealer does not:
- Connect to any backend servers
- Send data over the internet
- Use cloud storage
- Integrate with social media or tracking services
- Require internet connectivity to function

## Children's Privacy

Comp Dealer is safe for all ages. Since we don't collect any data, there are no special protections needed for children.
